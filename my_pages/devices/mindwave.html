Applications

There are several available applications to connect to Mindwave. In these works, the eegID application was always used.

The eegID application [2] connects to the Mindwave device via Bluetooth allowing the visualization and recording of specific data, Figure 3, namely:

  • PoorSignal
  • EEG raw data
  • Attention level
  • Concentration level
  • Blinking
  • Delta (1-3 Hz)
  • Theta (4-7 Hz)
  • Alpha Low (8-9 Hz)
  • Alpha High (10-12 Hz)
  • Beta Low (13-17 Hz)
  • Beta High (18-30 Hz)
  • Gamma Low (31-40 Hz)
  • Gamma Mid (41-50 Hz)
